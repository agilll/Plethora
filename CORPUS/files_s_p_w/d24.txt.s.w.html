His mother was Atossa, the daughter of Cyrus Cyrus_the_Great (who founded the Achaemenid Empire). He was, therefore, accepted as a great king before having to prove himself so in any way. Xerxes I Xerxes_I is celebrated for his many building projects throughout his empire but is best known, in both ancient and modern sources, for the massive expedition he mounted against Greece Ancient_Greece in 480 BCE Common_Era which, according to Herodotus, assembled the largest and most well equipped fighting force ever put into the field up to that point.
