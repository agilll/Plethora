Vastly outnumbered, the Greeks Ancient_Greece delayed the enemy in one of the most famous last stands of history. A small force led by King Leonidas Leonidas_I of Sparta blocked the only road through which the massive army of Xerxes I Xerxes_I could pass. The Persians The_Persians succeeded in defeating the Greeks Ancient_Greece but sustained heavy losses, incredibly disproportionate to those of the Greeks Ancient_Greece.
